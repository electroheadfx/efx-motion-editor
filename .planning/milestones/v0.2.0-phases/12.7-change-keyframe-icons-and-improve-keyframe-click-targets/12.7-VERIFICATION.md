## Human Verification Required

### 1. Losange shape for linear keyframes

**Test:** Open the app, create a content layer with keyframes, set interpolation to "Linear" on a keyframe, observe the timeline marker shape.
**Expected:** A gold diamond/rhombus shape (four equal sides, points at top/bottom/left/right) with color #E5A020.
**Why human:** Canvas 2D rendering cannot be observed programmatically without a browser environment.

### 2. Full circle for ease-in-out keyframes

**Test:** Set a keyframe's interpolation to "Ease In-Out" and observe the timeline marker.
**Expected:** A filled gold circle, slightly smaller than the losange bounding box (radius = iconSize * 0.7 = 6.3px).
**Why human:** Canvas rendering requires browser inspection.

### 3. Left half-circle for ease-in keyframes

**Test:** Set a keyframe's interpolation to "Ease In" and observe the timeline marker.
**Expected:** Left half of the circle filled gold, right half empty, with a full circle outline.
**Why human:** Canvas rendering requires browser inspection.

### 4. Right half-circle for ease-out keyframes

**Test:** Set a keyframe's interpolation to "Ease Out" and observe the timeline marker.
**Expected:** Right half of the circle filled gold, left half empty, with a full circle outline.
**Why human:** Canvas rendering requires browser inspection.

### 5. Crosshair cursor and glow on hover

**Test:** Move the cursor over a keyframe icon without clicking.
**Expected:** Cursor changes to crosshair; the icon shows a subtle brighter glow (shadowBlur 3 with #E5A020 shadow). Moving away removes both effects.
**Why human:** Cursor state and canvas shadow rendering require live browser observation.

### 6. Nearest-wins click selection

**Test:** Place two keyframes 2-3 frames apart on the timeline. Click at a point visually between them but closer to one.
**Expected:** The keyframe whose pixel center is closest to the click point becomes selected (highlighted in #FFD700), not the first one in the list.
**Why human:** Requires hands-on interaction to confirm spatial hit-test behavior.

### 7. Icon size fixed at 9px regardless of zoom

**Test:** Zoom the timeline in to maximum, observe keyframe icons. Zoom out to minimum, observe again.
**Expected:** Icons appear the same 9px visual size at all zoom levels (they are drawn in pixel space, not frame-proportional space).
**Why human:** Visual size comparison across zoom levels requires browser interaction.
