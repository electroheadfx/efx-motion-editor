## Decisions Made

- Generated interpolation metadata should carry source-neighbor provenance directly on cache entries so UI, persistence, and playback can distinguish render-only derived frames from authoritative real keys.
- The store accepts visible `hold` and `alpha-blend` inputs defensively, normalizing them to the existing persisted `duplicate`/`blend` contract without adding new persisted modes.
- Replacement regeneration should use `_tryRegenerateGeneratedRotoCache`, matching upsert/remove semantics and preserving D-11 failure safety.
