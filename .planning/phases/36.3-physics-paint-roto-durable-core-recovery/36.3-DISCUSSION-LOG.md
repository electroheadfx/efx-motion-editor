## Close-unsaved behavior

| Option | Description | Selected |
|--------|-------------|----------|

**Notes:** Closing must never auto-save or block. Unsaved edits after last Save current are lost, but previously saved cached frame remains intact. Close prompt/save-on-close belongs to a later MVP slice.
