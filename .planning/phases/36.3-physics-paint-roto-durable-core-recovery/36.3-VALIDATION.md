## Coverage Notes

`app/src/lib/physicPaintRotoDurableCore.test.ts` contains one vertical behavior test, `saves one current Roto frame as durable cache, reopens it as reference, and discards later unsaved edits`, covering:

- rendered `Save current` interaction and one `apply-canvas` payload for `phys-layer-1` frame 8;
- same-frame save with frame 9 left empty;
- parent `physicPaintStore.getFrame` visibility;
- `savePhysicPaintData('/project', outputs)` cache_path PNG persistence without inline data URLs;
- `loadPhysicPaintData('/project', persistedOutputs)` hydration back to runtime PNG data;
- `roto_cache_metadata.source` preserved as `real-key`;
- `createPhysicPaintLaunchContext(..., 'roto')` exposing the saved frame as a cached Roto reference without editable stroke restore;
- cached reference staying out of engine paper background and clearing stale editable strokes;
- `beforeunload` close behavior not blocking, prompting, or sending another apply payload;
- saved cache integrity after later unsaved edits;
- strict minimal Roto surface copy and absence of deferred interpolation/key/playback controls.
