# Phase 12.7 Plan 01: Change Keyframe Icons Summary

**Interpolation-aware keyframe icons (losange/circle/half-circles) with 18px nearest-wins hit area and crosshair hover glow**

## Accomplishments
- Four distinct keyframe icon shapes render based on interpolation type: losange (linear), full circle (ease-in-out), left half-circle (ease-in), right half-circle (ease-out)
- Icon size increased from 6px to 9px with 18px invisible hit area for generous click targets
- Nearest-wins selection logic replaces first-match, so clicking between two close keyframes selects the closest one
- Hover feedback: crosshair cursor and subtle gold glow (#F0B830 fill, shadowBlur 3) on keyframe hover
- Gold color scheme preserved: normal #E5A020, hover #F0B830, selected #FFD700 with white stroke

## Task Commits

## Files Created/Modified
- `Application/src/components/timeline/TimelineRenderer.ts` - Added drawLosange, drawCircle, drawHalfCircleLeft, drawHalfCircleRight, drawKeyframeIcon dispatcher, setHoveredKeyframe setter; replaced drawDiamond; increased icon size to 9px
- `Application/src/components/timeline/TimelineInteraction.ts` - Expanded hit threshold from 8px to 18px, nearest-wins bestHit logic, hover state tracking with crosshair cursor

## Decisions Made
- Icon size 9px fixed (not zoom-scaled) with 18px invisible hit area -- provides generous click targets without visual clutter
- Nearest-wins selection replaces first-match for overlapping keyframes -- more intuitive when keyframes are close together
- Hover glow uses shadowBlur 3 with #E5A020 (distinct from selected state's shadowBlur 6 with #FFD700) -- subtle but noticeable distinction
