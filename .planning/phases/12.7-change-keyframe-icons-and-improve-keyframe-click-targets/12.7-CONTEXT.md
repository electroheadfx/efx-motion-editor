# Phase 12.7: Change Keyframe Icons and Improve Keyframe Click Targets - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-03-18
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Change keyframe markers on the timeline canvas from uniform gold diamonds to interpolation-aware icons (losange, full circle, half circles) and improve click target generosity. No new keyframe capabilities — this is visual and interaction polish only.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Icon shape by interpolation type
- **Linear interpolation:** losange (diamond/rhombus) shape
- **Cubic ease (middle keyframe):** full circle
- **Ease in:** left half-circle (left side filled, right side empty — fill matches easing direction)
- **Ease out:** right half-circle (right side filled, left side empty — fill matches easing direction)

### Icon colors
- Keep the existing gold color scheme: normal #E5A020, selected #FFD700
- Same highlight treatment across all four shapes: bright gold fill, white stroke, subtle glow when selected

### Icon size
- Increase from current 6px to 8-10px range for better visibility and clickability
- Fixed pixel size — does NOT scale with zoom level

### Click target sizing
- Invisible hit area extends beyond the visual icon boundary (e.g., icon is 10px but hit area is ~16px) for easier clicking, especially at low zoom
- Nearest-wins for overlapping keyframes — click selects the closest keyframe to the cursor

### Hover & feedback
- Subtle highlight on hover: brighten the icon slightly or add a faint glow when cursor is over a keyframe
- Crosshair cursor when hovering over a keyframe on the timeline
- No tooltip — info is available in the sidebar when selected

### Claude's Discretion
- Exact pixel sizes for icon and hit area within the decided ranges
- Canvas rendering approach for half-circle shapes (arc + fill vs path)
- Hover highlight intensity and animation

</decisions>
