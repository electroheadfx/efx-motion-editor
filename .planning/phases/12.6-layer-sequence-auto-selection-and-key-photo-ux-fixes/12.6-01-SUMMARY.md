# Phase 12.6 Plan 01: Layer Auto-Selection and Key Photo UX Summary

**Layer auto-selection on sequence switch, key photo click-to-seek with frame-derived highlight ring and auto-scroll strip**

## Accomplishments
- Pure helper modules (getTopLayerId, getActiveKeyPhotoIndex) with 14 TDD unit tests
- Switching sequences auto-selects top-most layer, showing transform overlay handles
- Clicking key photo seeks playhead AND selects top-most layer (bidirectional sync)
- Key photo highlight ring tracks displayFrame position (not separate selectedKeyPhotoId)
- Strip auto-scrolls on scrub/nav but NOT during playback
- Empty active sequences show clickable "+ Add photos" prompt

## Decisions Made

- Auto-scroll suppressed during playback via isPlaying.peek() gate -- fires naturally on playback stop when displayFrame syncs
