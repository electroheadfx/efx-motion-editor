## Wave 0 Requirements

Existing infrastructure covers all Phase 36.1 requirements.

- `app/src/types/physicPaint.test.ts` covers typed payload, launch context, frame-sync, and saved Play range validation.
- `app/src/stores/physicPaintStore.test.ts` covers multi-range store semantics, overlap rejection, version bumps, and persistence round-trip.
- `app/src/lib/physicPaintBridge.test.ts` covers scrubber-aware launch behavior and browser/native bridge validation.
- `app/src/components/timeline/TimelineRenderer.test.ts` covers nested marker geometry, graphics-only rendering contracts, and live layout marker threading.
- `app/src/components/physic-paint/PhysicsPaintWorkflowStrip.test.ts` and `app/src/components/physic-paint/PhysicsPaintStudio.test.ts` cover standalone local preview and cache/remake behavior.
- `packages/efx-physic-paint/src/animation/AnimationPlayer.test.ts` covers sequential one-hand animation allocation.
