## Decisions Made

- Kept marker editing out of timeline rendering; timeline marker data is read-only layout state backed by validated store ranges.
- Used `physicPaintVersion.value` as the marker recomputation trigger so Save play, clear/remake, delete, and project load mutations can refresh timeline markers.
- Split current-frame state into `timelineFrameSignal.ts` rather than importing `timelineStore` inside `frameMap.ts`, avoiding a circular dependency.
