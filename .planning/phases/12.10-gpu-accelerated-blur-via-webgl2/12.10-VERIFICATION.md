## Notable Implementation Detail: Y-flip Fix

The plan did not specify Y-flip handling, but Plan 02 introduced a post-checkpoint fix (commit `6cbab21`) to address the WebGL bottom-up vs Canvas top-down coordinate mismatch. The fix uses `gl.pixelStorei(gl.UNPACK_FLIP_Y_WEBGL, true)` during `texSubImage2D` upload (glBlur.ts lines 332-335) rather than a vertex shader flip. This is the standard approach and is correctly implemented. Additionally, `globalCompositeOperation = 'copy'` on readback (line 362-363) prevents double-compositing.
