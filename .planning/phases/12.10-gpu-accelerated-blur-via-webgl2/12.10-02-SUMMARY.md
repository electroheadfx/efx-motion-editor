**1. [Rule 1 - Bug] Fixed GPU blur Y-flip and double-compositing bugs**
- **Found during:** Task 2 (visual verification)
- **Issue:** GPU blur output was vertically flipped (WebGL Y-axis is bottom-up vs Canvas top-down) and blur was being composited twice (once from GPU readback, once from renderer)
- **Fix:** Added Y-flip in the vertex shader and fixed the compositing pipeline to avoid double-application
- **Files modified:** Application/src/lib/glBlur.ts
- **Verification:** Visual verification confirmed correct blur rendering without flip or double-compositing artifacts
- **Committed in:** 6cbab21
