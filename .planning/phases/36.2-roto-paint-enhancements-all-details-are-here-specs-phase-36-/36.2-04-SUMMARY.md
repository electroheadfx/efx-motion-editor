## Decisions Made

- Generated interpolation frames remain render-only in launch semantics; when opened from EFX Motion they point the standalone at a real Roto key rather than becoming editable cells.
- Missing Roto background-only behavior lives in PreviewRenderer so export inherits it through existing `renderer.renderFrame()` delegation.
- The implementation avoids new dependencies, engine imports, stroke replay, or any batch/headless physics paint path.
