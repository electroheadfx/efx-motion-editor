## Decisions Made

- Current-frame dirty detection remains `workflowMode === 'roto' && dirtyRotoFramesRef.current.has(currentFrame)` after snapshotting; pending non-current frames are not part of this MVP.
- `Close without saving` does not call save, flush, apply, or delete paths; it only bypasses the close guard and closes.
- `Close saving` records the operation id returned by `saveRotoFrame(null)` and closes only from `handleApplyResult` when that operation succeeds.
