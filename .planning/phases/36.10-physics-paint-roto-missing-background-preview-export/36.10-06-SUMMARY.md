## Decisions Made

- Persisted Roto background metadata now crosses the standalone launch boundary as `rotoBackground`, validated by `isPhysicPaintRotoBackgroundMetadata`.
- Real-key paper backgrounds are drawn dynamically in PreviewRenderer, not baked into alpha-only Roto PNG caches.
- ExportRenderer remains free of Roto replay/missing-frame logic and inherits visual parity by delegating to PreviewRenderer.
- Active-frame saves reuse one transparent capture; non-active flushes still use the safe state-restore fallback path.
