## Decisions Made

- Used explicit `source: 'background-only-support'` because `source: 'real-key'` plus `backgroundOnly: true` could not safely distinguish derived render support from editable painted Roto keys.
- Recompute accepts explicit requested frames and materializes only those with strict interior real-key spans, preserving Plan 01's dynamic leading/trailing resolver behavior.
- Support cleanup remains separate from generated interpolation cleanup so generated frame behavior and Phase 36.9 real-key playback helpers stay isolated.
