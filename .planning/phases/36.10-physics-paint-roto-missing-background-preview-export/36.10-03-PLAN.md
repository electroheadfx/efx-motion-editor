**As a** stop-motion animator, **I want to** render missing Roto frames as transparent or paper-background-only frames, **so that** gaps between painted keys and trailing frames preview/export consistently without accidental paint content.

Purpose: Users need to trust the timeline/status surface while preview/export rules run; the UI must explain what is happening without becoming a new mode system.
Output: Tested workflow-state/session/UI contracts for `Background only`, transparent missing status, exact replacement success, and no new controls.
</objective>
