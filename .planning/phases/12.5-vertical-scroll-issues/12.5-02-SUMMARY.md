## Accomplishments

## Files Created/Modified

## Decisions Made
- Deselect sidebar sequence selection (`uiStore.selectSequence(null)`) when playback starts to avoid triggering sidebar re-renders every frame
- Tick loop only calls `timelineStore.ensureTrackVisible(entry.sequenceId)` -- no `sequenceStore.setActive` or `uiStore.selectSequence` during playback
- `syncActiveSequence()` in `stop()` re-syncs sidebar to current playhead position after playback ends
