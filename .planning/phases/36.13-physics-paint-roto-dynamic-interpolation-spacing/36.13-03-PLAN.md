<objective>
Make Roto key utilities preserve intentional custom timing when source keys move and remove ambiguous timing when source keys are deleted.

Purpose: Custom spans must follow real-key sequence edits without turning generated frames into editable inputs or leaving stale endpoint references.
Output: Tested controller/session transactions for override rebase, delete drop, paste far-empty creation, and generated-frame copy blocking.
</objective>

**As a** stop-motion animator, **I want to** intentionally distant real Roto keys to create remembered per-segment interpolation spacing, **so that** custom timing survives global in-between count changes, interpolation toggles, save/load, preview, and export.
