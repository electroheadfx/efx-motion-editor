## Gaps Summary

One gap blocks full compliance with the ROADMAP success criterion:

**Gap: FullSpeedBadge text and position (FS-03 / ROADMAP criterion #2)**

The `FullSpeedBadge` component was intentionally redesigned during plan execution (fix commit `af3bed7`, "badge to controls bar"). The original plan called for an absolute-positioned "⚡ FULL SPEED" text badge overlaid on the canvas (mirroring SpeedBadge). The delivered implementation is a small inline ⚡ icon in the controls bar with no text label.

This change is documented in the Plan 02 SUMMARY but not in Plan 01 SUMMARY (which incorrectly claims "badge on canvas"). The ROADMAP success criterion ("A FULL SPEED badge appears on the canvas") was not updated to reflect this decision.

**Resolution options:**
1. Add "FULL SPEED" text back to the badge and reposition it as a canvas overlay to match the ROADMAP criterion.
2. Accept the current controls-bar design and update the ROADMAP criterion + plan acceptance criteria to reflect the intentional change.

The gap is a documentation/alignment issue rather than a functionality failure — full-speed mode is communicates via the controls bar icon.
