## Accomplishments
- GlTransition interface with shaderId, params, duration, curve fields added to Sequence type
- Dual-texture WebGL2 rendering pipeline (renderGlslTransition) with from/to texture upload, progress clamping, and per-param uniform binding

## Decisions Made
- GlTransition is a separate interface (not overloading Transition type) for clean mutual exclusion with crossDissolve per D-02
- Transition programs cached with 'transition:' prefix key to avoid cache collision with generator/fx-image programs
- Progress clamped to [0.001, 0.999] per Pitfall 7 to avoid shader edge-case artifacts
- Vec2 uniform params decomposed to paired float params (e.g. directionX/directionY) with auto-reconstruction in buildTransitionFragmentSource wrapper, avoiding modifications to the ShaderParamDef system
