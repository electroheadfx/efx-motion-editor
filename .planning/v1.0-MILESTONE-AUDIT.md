### Remaining Integration Issues (Medium Priority)

1. **appConfig.ts missing `store.save()` calls** — `addRecentProject()` and `setLastProjectPath()` call `LazyStore.set()` but never `store.save()`. Recent projects list could be lost on hard crash. Affects PROJ-05, PROJ-06.

2. **`projectStore.closeProject()` missing `layerStore.reset()`** — Stale mock layer data persists across project sessions. Cosmetic in v1.0 (layers are mock). Becomes real bug when Phase 5 adds real layers.
